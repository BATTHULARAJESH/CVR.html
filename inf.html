Campus

The College is spread over a built-up area of over 36,000 sqm. on a sprawling 33-acre campus amidst verdant surroundings.

The College has

  • 95 spacious classrooms and 119 labs (all classrooms and labs are equipped with LCD projectors)
  • Air-conditioned conference rooms
  • An independent three storied Library building
  • An independent four storied Student Centre (with canteen, gym, indoor sports, placement cell)
  • Auditorium (seating capacity: 1500, fully air-conditioned, equipped with WiFi and 4 high powered LCD projectors)
  • Extensive outdoor sports facilities
  • Hostel
  • Health Centre

All laboratories are supported by UPS and continuous power back-up from captive generators (> 500kVA).
